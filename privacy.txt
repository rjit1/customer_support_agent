# GURTOY PRIVACY POLICY & DATA PROTECTION

## Privacy Policy Overview
At Gurtoy (www.thegurtoys.com), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, shop with us, or interact with our customer service.

## Information We Collect:

### Personal Information:
- Name and contact details (email, phone number)
- Shipping and billing addresses
- Payment information for order processing
- Order history and preferences
- Communication records with customer service

### Non-Personal Information:
- Website usage data and browsing patterns
- Device information and IP address
- Cookies for enhanced user experience
- Location data (with permission) for delivery services

## How We Use Your Information:

### Order Processing & Customer Service:
- Process and fulfill your toy orders
- Communicate about order status and delivery
- Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
- Handle returns, exchanges, and warranty claims

### Service Improvement:
- Personalize your shopping experience
- Recommend age-appropriate toys and products
- Improve our website functionality and user interface
- Analyze shopping patterns to enhance our inventory

### Marketing Communications (Optional):
- Send promotional emails about new toy arrivals
- Share special offers and seasonal discounts
- Notify about toy safety recalls or important updates
- Invite participation in customer surveys

## Information Sharing & Protection:

### We DO NOT:
- Sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties
- Share customer data with unauthorized parties
- Use your information for purposes other than stated

### We MAY Share Information With:
- Trusted shipping partners for order delivery
- Payment processors for secure transaction handling
- Legal authorities if required by law
- Service providers who help operate our website (under strict confidentiality)

## Data Security Measures:
- SSL encryption for all online transactions
- Secure servers and regular security updates
- Limited access to customer information
- Regular monitoring for unauthorized access
- Safe disposal of physical records

## Your Rights & Choices:
- Access and review your personal information
- Update or correct your account details
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Request deletion of your account data
- Contact us for any privacy concerns

## Children's Privacy:
As a toy store, we care deeply about children's privacy. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Parents can review and request deletion of their child's information.

## Cookie Policy:
We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and provide personalized product recommendations. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

## Updates to Privacy Policy:
Gurtoy reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on our website with the effective date. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of any updates.
